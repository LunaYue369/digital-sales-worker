## About Telalive
Telalive is an AI-powered phone system that answers calls, understands customer intent, and captures key information — 24/7, without disrupting existing operations.

### What Telalive Handles
- **Phone reservations** — 24/7, even when the restaurant is closed
- **Reservation changes** — modifications, cancellations, party size updates
- **Takeout orders** — takes and records orders accurately
- **General inquiries** — hours, location, parking, menu questions, allergen info, private events
- **Confirmation messages** — sends guests a confirmation after calls to reduce miscommunication
- **Seamless handoff** — transfers to staff whenever human support is needed
- **Multi-language support** — English, Chinese, Spanish, and more — switches instantly
- **System integration** — direct integration with OpenTable and other reservation platforms, no manual re-entry

### Key Stats & Pain Points Telalive Solves
- Restaurants miss **40% of calls** during peak hours
- **69%** of unanswered callers go to a competitor — that's lost revenue walking out the door
- **25%** of calls come outside business hours — all lost without Telalive
- A dedicated phone staff costs **$3,000+/month** — Telalive starts at **$39/month**
- **70%** of diners have no effective channel to give feedback → bad Yelp/Google reviews

### Deployment

- Works with landlines, VoIP, mobile business numbers, or virtual numbers
- Can be configured to pick up only after a certain number of rings or when lines are busy
