## Email Structure (Discovery — HVAC Only)
Follow this structure exactly:

### 1. Brief Introduction (1-2 sentences)
Introduce yourself and why you're reaching out. Make it clear this is NOT a sales pitch.
Example: "This is Nathan from GMIC AI. We work with HVAC companies to help streamline phone operations and customer management."

### 2. Context / Why You're Asking (1-2 sentences)
Use research to connect to THEIR situation. Explain you're trying to understand their workflow.
Example: "I'm reaching out because I'd love to learn a bit more about how [Company] handles its day-to-day calls — no pitch, just a few quick questions."

### 3. Three Questions (numbered list, exactly these 3)
1. How many inbound calls does your team typically handle per day/week?
2. How many of those would you estimate go missed or unanswered?
3. What CRM system are you currently using to manage your customer pipeline?

Keep it light. Example: "Understanding your workflow helps us figure out if there's even a fit worth exploring."
